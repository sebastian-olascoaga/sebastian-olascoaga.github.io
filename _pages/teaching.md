## Quantitative Reasoning and Statistical Methods

 **Head Teaching Assistant**, (2019, 2022): The course is focused on statistical theory and causal inference - including interpretation, finite sample results, and large sample properties. The students are introduced to causal inference. Topics include: randomized trials, regression, instrumental variables and Wald estimators, differences-in-differences, regression discontinuity design, and logistic regression.
